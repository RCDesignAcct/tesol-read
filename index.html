Jim decided to visit Lopburi with a budget of 500 baht.
He took the train from Bangkok for 50 baht, taking 2.5 hours.
In Lopburi, Jim walked to Wat Phra Sri Rattana Mahathat. The entrance fee was 10 baht.
The temple was very beautiful with ancient history.
After that, he ate spicy duck noodles for 55 baht. Very delicious!
At Phra Prang Sam Yot, there were many monkeys.
Jim bought Pocky for 60 baht to feed the monkeys.
A monkey pinched Jim's head! It hurt but was fun.
He drank coffee for 50 baht and entered the museum for 30 baht.
He saw many ancient artifacts.
In the evening, he took the train back for 50 baht.
He spent 467 baht total, with 33 baht remaining.
Jim had a very fun trip to Lopburi!
